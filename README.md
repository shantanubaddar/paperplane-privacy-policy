# PaperPlane Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 16, 2026

PaperPlane is a local file transfer app for nearby Android devices. This policy
explains what PaperPlane does and does not collect.

## Summary

PaperPlane does not require an account, does not show ads, does not use
analytics, and does not upload your files to a cloud server. Files are sent
directly between nearby devices on the same Wi-Fi or hotspot network.

## Information We Do Not Collect

PaperPlane does not collect, sell, share, or upload:

- Your files
- Your contacts
- Your photos or videos
- Your location
- Your messages
- Your browsing history
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics or tracking data

PaperPlane does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

## File Transfers

When you choose files to send, PaperPlane transfers those files directly to the
selected nearby device over the local network. Files are encrypted during
transfer and verified after the transfer finishes.

PaperPlane does not send your files to PaperPlane servers. PaperPlane does not
store copies of your files outside your device and the receiving device.

Received files are saved on the receiving device in:

```text
Downloads/PaperPlane
```

## Local Device Data

PaperPlane stores some information locally on your device so the app can work
properly:

- Trusted, unknown, and blocked device states
- Flight Log transfer history
- App settings such as light/dark mode and onboarding state
- Local cryptographic keys used for encrypted transfers

This information stays on your device. PaperPlane does not upload it to a
server.

## Permissions

PaperPlane may request or use the following Android permissions:

- **Notifications**: used to show incoming transfer requests and received-file
  alerts.
- **Network access**: used to transfer files directly over the local network.
- **Wi-Fi/network state**: used to discover nearby devices on the same Wi-Fi or
  hotspot network.
- **Multicast access**: used to make local device discovery more reliable.
- **Foreground service/data sync**: used to keep Receiving mode available while
  PaperPlane is running in the background.

PaperPlane does not request access to contacts, camera, microphone, SMS, or
broad storage.

## Device Trust and Blocking

PaperPlane lets you trust, untrust, or block nearby devices. Trusting a device
uses your device's biometric, PIN, pattern, or password confirmation when
available. Blocked devices cannot send files to you through PaperPlane.

Trust and block choices are stored locally on your device.

## Children

PaperPlane is a general utility app and is not directed specifically at
children. PaperPlane does not knowingly collect personal information from
children.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated when PaperPlane changes. The updated version
will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
